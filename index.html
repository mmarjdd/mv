Relationship Definition

For purposes of this Contract, the term "RELATIONSHIP" shall refer to the shared relational system known as "US," encompassing the emotional, practical, and life integration built and continuously developed by the PARTNERS.


The RELATIONSHIP is not a task, obligation, or temporary arrangement, but a living system of connection, growth, communication, and shared experience that evolves through time.

Know all persons by these presents

This Contract is made and entered into this 1st day of May 2026, at Angeles City, Pampanga, being the night where the lives of the PARTNERS formally intertwine, by and between:

VENCER G. PASCUAL, RCE, PE-1, SO-2

Hereinafter referred to collectively as the "PARTNERS."

Witnesseth
  • WHEREAS, the PARTNERS first established connection on December 1, 2025;
  • WHEREAS, the PARTNERS personally met on December 6, 2025, marking a meaningful first connection;
  • WHEREAS, on December 19, 2025, mutual intention to pursue the RELATIONSHIP was formally established — slowly, clearly, and without hesitation;
  • WHEREAS, the PARTNERS have successfully completed initial phases of development, including integration with respective families on: December 23, 2025 (Vencer's family) and January 10, 2026 (Marjorie's family);
  • WHEREAS, the foundation has been surveyed, tested, and proven stable through consistent interaction, shared experiences, and mutual effort;

NOW, THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the foregoing premises, the PARTNERS hereby agree as follows:

Article 1 · Contract Documents

The Contract Documents shall include all records, logs, conversations, memories, and shared experiences relating to the RELATIONSHIP, including but not limited to:

  • This Contract
  • Timeline and Monitoring Logs
  • Monthly Feedback Sessions
  • Shared Plans, Goals, and Commitments

Article 2 · Management

The PARTNERS shall jointly manage the RELATIONSHIP as equal contributors. Neither party shall diminish the independence, identity, or individuality of the other. The RELATIONSHIP exists to support, not replace, who each person already is.

Article 3 · Scope of Work

The PARTNERS agree to build, maintain, and continuously improve the RELATIONSHIP. All development shall be intentional, mutual, and respectful, including:

  • Honest and consistent communication — even when it is difficult, we will try, babe
  • Emotional presence and support
  • Shared time and lived experiences
  • Integration into each other's personal and family systems

These are not obligations, but small, consistent ways we choose to maintain how we show up for each other.


The PARTNERS further commit to building a RELATIONSHIP that does not require either to shrink, change beyond authenticity, or lose themselves for the sake of the other.


The PARTNERS also look forward to building shared experiences together, including celebrating occasions, exploring places, engaging in activities, and creating meaningful firsts that form part of the growing history of the RELATIONSHIP.

Article 4 · General Requirements

  • 4.1 Each PARTNER shall provide effort, time, and emotional presence necessary for the stability of the RELATIONSHIP.
  • 4.2 Regular communication and feedback sessions shall be maintained.
  • 4.3 Accountability shall remain mutual and continuous.
  • 4.4 Growth — individual and shared — shall be supported throughout all stages of development.

Article 5 · Quality Control / Quality Assurance

All aspects of the RELATIONSHIP shall be governed by: Care, Honesty, Patience, Respect, Understanding, and Emotional safety.


This is meant to be a gentle and patient kind of love — one that grows over time, without force or pressure.

Article 6 · Performance of Remedial Works

In the event of conflict, misalignment, or misunderstanding:

  • Both PARTNERS shall engage in open and respectful communication
  • Issues shall be addressed directly, not avoided
  • Resolution shall prioritize understanding and repair

We choose to fix things together — not walk away from them, babe.

Article 7 · Time and Duration

This Contract shall commence on May 1, 2026, and shall remain in effect for an indefinite duration. There is no completion date, as the RELATIONSHIP is intended for continuous development, maintenance, and growth.


Instead of completion, the PARTNERS agree to conduct periodic assessment sessions, where they will:

  • Share realizations and emotional reflections
  • Evaluate their experience with one another
  • Identify strengths within the RELATIONSHIP
  • Point out areas for improvement and growth
  • Align expectations and future direction

The absence of a completion date is replaced by a commitment to continue choosing each other — again and again, through time, babe.

Article 8 · Changes in Work

The PARTNERS may introduce modifications, improvements, or adjustments at any time, provided that such changes are mutually agreed upon. All changes shall aim to enhance, not compromise, the integrity of the RELATIONSHIP.

Article 9 · Responsibilities of the Partners

Each PARTNER shall:

  • Act in good faith
  • Provide support when needed
  • Maintain respect and emotional safety
  • Protect the integrity of the RELATIONSHIP

Neither shall abandon the RELATIONSHIP without communication and sincere effort toward resolution.

Article 10 · Events of Default

Default may include: failure to communicate, emotional withdrawal without explanation, or neglect of agreed responsibilities. In such cases, both PARTNERS shall prioritize discussion and resolution.

Article 11 · Effects of Default

In the event of default: honest communication shall be initiated, responsibility shall be acknowledged, and restoration of the RELATIONSHIP shall be attempted. The intent shall always be repair, not termination.

Article 12 · Final Acceptance

I hope you feel loved around me, babe.


I hope this relationship never asks you to shrink, change, or lose yourself just to stay.


I hope we continue choosing each other — not just now, but again and again.


And more than anything, I hope I become the kind of love you never have to heal from.
